[Hook]
[Acoustic guitar strums softly, setting a warm midnight mood]
I can feel the tide pulling in your eyes
Taking me away where the shadows hide

[Smooth electric guitar enters with a steady, mellow groove]

[Bass guitar slides in, building a subtle anticipation]

I can feel the magic has just begun

[Chorus]
[Drums kick in with a soft but driving rhythm]

Catching me in everything I crave

[Post-Chorus]
[Electric guitar echoes softly]
Pulling me under, pulling me in
Where the surface ends, that’s where we begin

[Verse 2]
[Acoustic picking returns, keeping it intimate]
Midnight on the porch, shadows on your face
Every little touch is a steady grace
I’m a restless soul on a lonely road
But you give me a feeling like coming home

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Electric guitar echoes softly]

[Instrumentation returns full and lush, driving home the emotion]
